# Code of Conduct

BrainBar aims to be a welcoming, practical open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when discussing bugs or design tradeoffs.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Help keep examples, screenshots, logs, and fixtures public-safe.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private data, secrets, or someone else's vault content.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Pressuring maintainers for unpaid support outside the scope of the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainer through the repository.
